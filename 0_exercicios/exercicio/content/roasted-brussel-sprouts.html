Roasted Brussels Sprouts Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Brussels sprouts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp balsamic vinegar (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Trim the ends of the Brussels sprouts and cut them in half.
  3. Toss the Brussels sprouts with olive oil, salt, pepper, and garlic powder in a bowl.
  4. Spread them in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  5. Roast for 20-25 minutes, until golden brown.
  6. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar before serving, if desired.
