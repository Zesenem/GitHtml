Zucchini Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 medium zucchinis, grated
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x9-inch baking pan.
  2. Prepare the zucchini: Grate the zucchinis and squeeze out excess moisture using a clean towel or paper towels.
  3. Mix dry ingredients: In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
  4. Mix wet ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, sugar, oil, and vanilla until smooth.
  5. Combine: Gradually stir the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until just combined. Fold in the grated zucchini and optional walnuts or raisins.
  6. Bake: Pour the batter into the prepared pan and spread evenly. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  7. Cool & Serve: Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely. Enjoy as is, or dust with powdered sugar for a sweet touch!
